Welcome to Mindboggle!

The Mindboggle project's mission is to improve the accuracy, precision, and consistency of automated labeling and shape analysis of human brain image data. We promote open science by making all software, data, and documentation freely and openly available.


  • Software: label, extract features, and analyze shapes of human brain image data
  • Data: atlases, templates, and the largest collection of publicly available, manually labeled human brains!
  • Papers: articles and conference proceedings related to Mindboggle
  • People: core contributors to Mindboggle
